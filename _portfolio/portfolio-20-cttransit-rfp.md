## Project Overview

A full competitive RFP response developed for the State of Connecticut Department of Transportation to conduct a rider satisfaction study of the CTtransit fixed-route bus system spanning 183 bus routes across 10 service areas over a three-year contract period.

## Key Contributions

<div class="metric-card">
  <strong>RFP Response Design:</strong> Developed a 15-page RFP response for CTtransit rider satisfaction surveys covering 183 bus routes across 10 service areas over a three-year contract period.
</div>

<div class="metric-card">
  <strong>Statistical Sampling Plan:</strong> Designed a statistically valid sampling plan targeting 9,000 total surveys, with at least 385 responses per service area at 95 percent confidence and a plus-or-minus 5 percent margin of error.
</div>

<div class="metric-card">
  <strong>Mixed-Mode Instrument Design:</strong> Built a six-language mixed-mode survey system using paper, tablet, and QR-code collection channels, with ADA-compliant instruments and a structured three-wave field deployment plan.
</div>

<div class="metric-card">
  <strong>Budget Model:</strong> Prepared a $1.964M budget model that remained 1.8 percent under the $2.0M ceiling while estimating an average cost of $218 per completed interview.
</div>

## Technical Stack
